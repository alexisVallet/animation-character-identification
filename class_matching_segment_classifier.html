Computes a one to one relation between the segments of two images, where related segments are the most similar in the image. Runs in O(|A||B|) time and O(|A| + |B|) space where A and B are the two segmentations.

Parameters

lMaskmask of image lImage.
sSegfirst segmentation.
sImageimage segmented by sSeg.
sMaskmask of image sImage.
labelingFunctionssegment labeling functions and associated scale parameters to label segment with, and measure similarity between segments using the gaussian kernel.
ignoreLasttrue iff the function must ignore the last segment. Useful when the last segment is the background segment, which is of no interest.
